Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to fast before a blood test?

It depends on the test. Tests like Fasting Blood Sugar and Lipid Profile require 10-12 hours of fasting.

How can I get my reports?

You can download them from our website, get them via WhatsApp, or collect a physical copy from the center.

Is home collection available on Sundays?

Yes, our home collection service is available 7 days a week from 6:30 AM to 8:00 PM.
